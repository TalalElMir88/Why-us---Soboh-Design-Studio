Why us

Our Core Services

1. Concept Design:

Visioning & Feasibility: We collaborate to shape your vision, conduct feasibility studies, and use cutting-edge BIM for early-stage visualizations.
Preliminary Analysis: Our team evaluates site conditions and regulatory factors, creating detailed sketches and 3D models.

2. Developed Design:

Refinement: We enhance architectural, structural, and MEP systems, ensuring perfect spatial coordination.
Collaboration: Through BIM, we ensure interdisciplinary harmony and resolve conflicts before they arise.

3. Schematic Design:

Detailed Drawings: We craft precise schematic designs and comprehensive 3D models, integrating all building systems.
Systems Integration: Our BIM-driven approach ensures compliance with local codes and standards, reducing risks.

4. Detailed Design:

Final Documentation: We produce exact construction documents, utilizing BIM for accuracy and efficiency.
Construction Documentation: Our detailed plans and automated schedules streamline the construction process.

5. Tender Packages:

Tender Preparation: We compile precise tender documents, including bills of quantities and specifications.
Contractor Selection: We assist in evaluating and selecting the best contractors, using BIM to ensure competitive pricing.
